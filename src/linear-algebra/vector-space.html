벡터공간

벡터공간 공리

모든 $\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v},\mathbf{w} \in V$와 모든 스칼라 $k, l$에 대해 다음을 만족하는, 덧셈과 스칼라곱이 정의된 공집합이 아닌 집합 $V$를 벡터공간 , $V$의 원소를 벡터 라고 한다.

  1. $$\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{v} \in V$$
  2. $$\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{v}=\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{u}$$
  3. $$\mathbf{u}+(\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{w})=(\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{v})+\mathbf{w}$$
  4. $$\exists \mathbf{0} \in V \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \mathbf{u}+\mathbf{0}=\mathbf{u}$$
  5. $$\exists -\mathbf{u} \in V \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \mathbf{u}+(-\mathbf{u})=\mathbf{0}$$
  6. $$ k\mathbf{u} \in V$$
  7. $$k(\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{v})=k\mathbf{u}+k\mathbf{v}$$
  8. $$(k+l)\mathbf{u}=k\mathbf{u}+l\mathbf{u}$$
  9. $$k(l\mathbf{u})=(kl)\mathbf{u}$$
  10. $$1\mathbf{u}=\mathbf{u}$$

벡터공간의 성질

$\mathbf{u}, \mathbf{v}, \mathbf{w}$가 벡터공간 $V$의 벡터이고 $k$가 스칼라일 때, $\mathbf{u}$가 벡터공간 $V$의 벡터일 때, $\mathbf{u}$가 벡터공간 $V$의 벡터이고 $k$가 스칼라일 때, $$0\mathbf{u}=\mathbf{0} \tag{\href{#property-1-proof}{\text{증명}}}$$ $$k\mathbf{0}=\mathbf{0} \tag{\href{#property-2-proof}{\text{증명}}}$$ $$(-1)\mathbf{u}=-\mathbf{u} \tag{\href{#property-5-proof}{\text{증명}}}$$

덧셈 항등원 유일성

$$\exists ! \mathbf{0} \in V \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \mathbf{u} + \mathbf{0} = \mathbf{0} \tag{\href{#property-3-proof}{\text{증명}}}$$

덧셈역원 유일성

$$\exists ! \mathbf{v} \in V \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v} = \mathbf{0} \tag{\href{#property-4-proof}{\text{증명}}}$$

소거법칙

$$\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{w}=\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{w} \quad \Rightarrow \quad \mathbf{u}=\mathbf{v} \tag{\href{#property-6-proof}{\text{증명}}}$$

1 증명

$$0\mathbf{u}\href{#axiom-4}{=}0\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{0}$$ $$\phantom{0\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-5}{=}0\mathbf{u}+[0\mathbf{u}+(-0\mathbf{u})]$$ $$\phantom{0\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-3}{=}[0\mathbf{u}+0\mathbf{u}]+(-0\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{0\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-8}{=}[0+0]\mathbf{u}+(-0\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{0\mathbf{u}}=0\mathbf{u}+(-0\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{0\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-5}{=}\mathbf{0}$$

2 증명

$$k\mathbf{0}\href{#axiom-4}{=}k\mathbf{0}+\mathbf{0}$$ $$\phantom{k\mathbf{0}}\href{#axiom-5}{=}k\mathbf{0}+[k\mathbf{0}+(-k\mathbf{0})]$$ $$\phantom{k\mathbf{0}}\href{#axiom-3}{=}[k\mathbf{0}+k\mathbf{0}]+(-k\mathbf{0})$$ $$\phantom{k\mathbf{0}}\href{#axiom-7}{=}k[\mathbf{0}+\mathbf{0}]+(-k\mathbf{0})$$ $$\phantom{k\mathbf{0}}\href{#axiom-4}{=}k\mathbf{0}+(-k\mathbf{0})$$ $$\phantom{k\mathbf{0}}\href{#axiom-5}{=}\mathbf{0}$$

3 증명
전제

벡터공간 $V$의 벡터 $\mathbf{u} \in V$에 대해 두 벡터 $\mathbf{0}_1,\mathbf{0}_2 \in V$가 다음을 만족한다고 가정한다.

  1. $$\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{0}_1 = \mathbf{u}, \; \mathbf{0}_1 + \mathbf{u} = \mathbf{u}$$
  2. $$\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{0}_2 = \mathbf{u}, \; \mathbf{0}_2 + \mathbf{u} = \mathbf{u}$$
추론

$$\mathbf{0}_1\href{#property-3-proof-assumption-2}{=}\mathbf{0}_1+\mathbf{0}_2$$ $$\phantom{\mathbf{0}_1}\href{#property-3-proof-assumption-1}{=}\mathbf{0}_2$$ 두 벡터 $\mathbf{0}_1,\mathbf{0}_2$는 동일하다.

결론

벡터공간 $V$의 모든 벡터 $\mathbf{u} \in V$에 대해 $\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{0} = \mathbf{u}$를 만족하는 덧셈 항등원 벡터 $\mathbf{0} \in V$는 유일하다.

4 증명
전제

벡터공간 $V$의 벡터 $\mathbf{u} \in V$에 대해 두 벡터 $\mathbf{v},\mathbf{w} \in V$가 다음을 만족한다고 가정한다.

  1. $$\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v} = \mathbf{0}, \; \mathbf{v} + \mathbf{u} = \mathbf{0}$$
  2. $$\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{w} = \mathbf{0}, \; \mathbf{w} + \mathbf{u} = \mathbf{0}$$
추론

$$\mathbf{v}\href{#axiom-4}{=}\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{0}$$ $$\phantom{\mathbf{v}}\href{#property-4-proof-assumption-2}{=}\mathbf{v} + (\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{w})$$ $$\phantom{\mathbf{v}}\href{#axiom-3}{=}(\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{u}) + \mathbf{w}$$ $$\phantom{\mathbf{v}}\href{#property-4-proof-assumption-1}{=}\mathbf{0} + \mathbf{w}$$ $$\phantom{\mathbf{v}}\href{#axiom-2}{=}\mathbf{w} + \mathbf{0}$$ $$\phantom{\mathbf{v}}\href{#axiom-4}{=}\mathbf{w}$$ 두 벡터 $\mathbf{v},\mathbf{w}$는 동일하다.

결론

벡터공간 $V$의 모든 벡터 $\mathbf{u} \in V$에 대해 $\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{v} = \mathbf{0}$를 만족하는 덧셈 역원 벡터 $\mathbf{v} \in V$는 유일하다.

5 증명

$$(-1)\mathbf{u}\href{#axiom-4}{=}(-1)\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{0}$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-5}{=}(-1)\mathbf{u}+\{\mathbf{u}+(-\mathbf{u})\}$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-3}{=}\{(-1)\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{u}\}+(-\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-10}{=}\{(-1)\mathbf{u}+1\mathbf{u}\}+(-\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-8}{=}(-1+1)\mathbf{u}+(-\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}=0\mathbf{u}+(-\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}\href{#property-1}{=}\mathbf{0}+(-\mathbf{u})$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-2}{=}(-\mathbf{u})+\mathbf{0}$$ $$\phantom{(-1)\mathbf{u}}\href{#axiom-4}{=}-\mathbf{u}$$

6 증명

$$\phantom{\Rightarrow}\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{w}=\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{w}$$ $$\Rightarrow(\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{w})+(-\mathbf{w})=(\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{w})+(-\mathbf{w})$$ $$\href{#axiom-3}{\Rightarrow}\mathbf{u}+\{\mathbf{w}+(-\mathbf{w})\}=\mathbf{v}+\{\mathbf{w}+(-\mathbf{w})\}$$ $$\href{#axiom-5}{\Rightarrow}\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{0}=\mathbf{v}+\mathbf{0}$$ $$\href{#axiom-4}{\Rightarrow}\mathbf{u}=\mathbf{v}$$
